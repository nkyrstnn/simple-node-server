1973-1976

A packet radio network use radio waves to connect computers and in 1973, engineers began to look at ways to connect the ARPANET to the packet radio network.

1997

Satellite Network was joined with the PRNET and ARPANET by techinicians. They called it “inter-networking” or INTERNET for short. Soon other networks like USENET, BITNET, CSNET and NSFNET joined.

1990

Tim Berners Lee developed the WORLD WIDE WEB to simplify the navigation on the Internet. Soon after that, people started to mistakenly identify the internet and the world wide web as the same thing but the internet is a massive network and the world wide web is a way to navigate it.
