Bluberry Cheesecake

Ingredients

for crust

  • 2 1/3 cups of graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 unsalted butter melt
  • 1/4 cup sugar

for filling

  • 4 8-ounce packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 16-ounce container sour cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

for topping

  • 2 6-ounce basket fresh blueberries
  • 1/3 cup all-fruit blueberry spread

Preparation

Make Crust

  1. Position rack in center of oven and prehead to 375 F
  2. Wrap outside of 10-inch diameter springform pan with heavy duty foil
  3. Combine all ingredients in processor and blend using on/off turns until crumbs are moist
  4. press crumb mixture firmly onto bottom and 2 inches up sides of prepared pan
  5. bake until crust begins to brown, about 8 minutes, and transfer crust to rack and cool

Make Filling

  1. Using electric mixer, beat cream cheese and sugar in large bowl until well belnded
  2. Beat in flour and add eggs 1 at a time, beating just until combined
  3. beat in sour cream, milk, and vanilla
  4. Pour filling into crust
  5. Place springform pan in large roasting pan
  6. Pour enough hot water into roasting pan to come 1 inch up sides of pan
  7. Bake cheesecake until just set in center and top is slightly puffed and golden brown
  8. Turn off oven; keep door closed; let cheesecake stand in oven for 1 hour
  9. remove cheesecake from roasting pan and refrigerate until cold
  10. cover; refrigerate overnight

Make Topping

  1. Stir blueberry spread in large skillet over low heat until melted
  2. Remove from heat and add blueberries
  3. Spoon blueberry mixtures over top of cake, spreading evenly
  4. Chill until cold and transfer cake to platter
